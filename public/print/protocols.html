Protocols

AA-101

Small Fire

In case of small contained fire, attempt to diffuse it the fire extinguisher available in each room.

If the fire cannot be contained, refer to protocol AA-201.

AA-201

Large Fire

In case of large fire, trigger the nearest fire alarm switch. A switch is located in each hallway.

Switch off all lights and close all doors behind you.

Calmly make your way to the nearest exit.

Do not use the elevators.

AF-280

Ventilation Failure

In case the closed ventilation system for the facility suffers a failure or preventative shut-off, obtain an oxygen tank and mask from the storage cabinet in each lab.

Secure the mask over your nose and mouth ensuring a tight seal.

Turn the knob on the oxygen tank into the 'On' position.

Breath normally.

You will be notified via intercom when it is safe to remove the mask.

AI-527

Artificial Interference

In case of interference by artificial entities, report to security in person.

Do not use any electronic equipment including but not limited to: computers, telephone, lab equipment.

Do not speak

Do not use the elevators.

You will be required to use electronic swipe cards to navigate through the building. Do so swiftly and un-suspiciously.

EE-319

Electrical Overdose

In case of electrical overdose from malfunctioning equipment, contact security via telephone.

If the accident occurred in the lab, turn off lab power via the master switch.

Do not treat burns. Wait for medical personnel.

EF-621

Explosion

In case of explosion, follow protocol AA-201.

Proceed through the facility with caution as the building structure may have become unstable.

IA-327

Intruder Alert

In case of an unauthorized intruder, contact security via telephone.

Do not engage or pursue the intruder.

Take a moment to observe and remember their appearance, location and direction. This information will be valuable to security.

If possible, lock yourself in the nearest room.

KA-452

Infectious Agent

In case of accidental release of an infectious agent, you are required to immunize yourself and exit the building.

Retrieve the broad spectrum antidote from the closest medical dispensary.

The antidote will provide protection against any infectious agent for a period of 24 hours after injection.

The antidote will cure any infection if administered within 12 hours of infection.

If possible, provide assistance to fellow employees.

No employee will be allowed to exit the facility without proof of immunization.

GL-006

Gaseous Leak

In case of accidental release of a gaseous substance, swiftly retrieve the gas mask in the storage cabinet found in every room and hallway entrance.

Secure the mask over your nose and mouth ensuring a tight seal.

Pull the emergency alarm and enter code: 006.

Calmly make your way to the nearest exit.

ME-010

Mental Breakdown

In case an employee suffers from a mental breakdown, assess the situation.

If the employee is non-violent and does not have a weapon, attempt to diffuse them with calm and reassuring words.

If the employee has the possibility to injure themselves or others, attempt to quarantine the employee in a locked room.

Security will be there shortly with tranquilizers to subdue the employee.

PE-874

Patient Escape

In case a patient from the mental health ward escape, notify security via telephone immediately.

If possible, attempt to quarantine the patient in a locked room.

Do not attempt to pursue or catch the patient yourself.

Security will be there shortly with tranquilizers to subdue the patient.

PP-490

Poisoning

In case a accidental poisoning, contact security via telephone immediately.

Do not eat or drink anything further. Do not induce vomiting.

Remain calm. Medical personnel will bring immediate treatment.

RA-983

Radiation Exposure

In case of accidental exposure to radiation, quarantine the affected individual(s) in the nearest enclosed space.

Pull the emergency alarm and enter code: 983.

Turn off lights in the quarantined room

Remain calm. Medical personnel will bring immediate treatment.

TT-109

Temporal Dislocation

In case of accidental exposure to flux particles resulting in temporal dislocation, remain at your current location.

Once security identifies that you have missed your last security check-in, your last location will be investigated.

As part of the search procedure, we will sweep alternate phase and timelines.

WO-027

Wardrobe Dysfunction

In case of accidental destruction of current day's clothing, spare scrubs and lab coats are found in the storage cabinet of each lab.

WW-027

Flooding

In case of facility flooding, retrieve the oxygen tank and mask from the storage cabinet of each lab.

Secure the mask over your eyes and nose. Secure the tube adapter in your mouth.

Turn the knob on the oxygen tank into the 'On' position.

Breath through your mouth.

Swim towards the nearest exit.
